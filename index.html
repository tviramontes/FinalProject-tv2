Dallas Cowboys

The Dallas Cowboys were the first team I was ever a fan of. My mom was a Cowboys fan so I began to watch them and like them when I was younger.

The Cowboys are known as America's team due to their popularity throughout the country. They are one of the most valuable sports teams in the world, as well as one of the most succesful teams in NFL history.

Chicago Cubs

2016 World Series Champions





They were known as the loveable losers for quite some time, before finally winning a championship again in 2016 after a 108 year drought.









In a 7 games series with the Cleveland Indians, the cubs went down 3 games to 1, by coming back to win the entire series, they are one of only 5 teams in league history to accomplish this feat.

Chicago Blackhawks

They won three of these titles in 5 years from 2010-2015. During this time I was lucky enough to go to a few of their games, and be able to hold the Stanley Cup after their 2013 win.
